Welcome to PICT

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) is a premier institute for computer education in India. Established in 1983, PICT has consistently been ranked among the top computer engineering institutes in the country.

Our state-of-the-art campus provides an ideal environment for learning and research. With world-class faculty and cutting-edge facilities, we strive to nurture the next generation of computer professionals.

Our Achievements

Over the years, PICT has achieved numerous milestones:

  • Ranked #1 in Computer Engineering by National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF)
  • Recipient of the Best Engineering College Award by the Government of Maharashtra
  • Produced several industry leaders and entrepreneurs
  • Active involvement in research and development projects
